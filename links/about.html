About Us

The interventions offered are:

  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Compassionate Focused Approach (CAT)
  • Mindfulness and integrating Art and Music therapy
  • Schema based Psychotherapy

  • Eating Disorders

  • Depression
  • Anxiety : Worry Management

  • OCD
  • Low Self-esteem
  • Parenting Difficulties
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder
